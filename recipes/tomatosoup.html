Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Saute onion and garlic in the hot oil until onion is translucent, about 10 minutes.
  2. Stir tomatoes, chicken broth, black pepper, basil, and oregano into onion mixture; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until flavors have blended, about 20 minutes.
  3. Remove saucepan from heat and let soup cool slightly.
  4. Puree soup in small batches in a blender or food processor until smooth. Return soup to saucepan and stir in cream. Heat soup over low heat until warmed through, about 5 minutes.
