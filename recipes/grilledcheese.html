Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

Grilled cheese is a simple dish consisting of two slices of bread with melted cheese in between. It is typically cooked on a griddle or frying pan until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted. Grilled cheese is a popular comfort food that is enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, on a griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Place the cheese slices on top of the bread.
  4. Top with the other slice of bread, butter side up.
  5. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Remove from heat and let cool for a minute before cutting and serving.
